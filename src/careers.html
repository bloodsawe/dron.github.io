Life at Edinburgh HQ

Located in southeastern Scotland, Edinburgh has been the Capital of Scotland since the 15th century and is recognised for its vast offering of heritage, culture, excitement and wonder.

Sparking creativity, Edinburgh has produced some of the most creative people in the world, such as J K Rowling who wrote the majority of Harry Potter books whilst living in the city. As the greenest city in the UK, Edinburgh is home to 112 of the countries most beautiful parks including Arthur's Seat, an extinct volcano which is the main peak of the group of hills that form most of Holyrood Park.

The Cyberhawk Edinburgh HQ, located a stone-throw from Edinburgh City, is a beautifully modern, flexible workspace based in the Edinburgh International Climbing Arena (EICA).

Best known for Edinburgh Castle which sit above the city on Castle Rock and is home to the royal Crown Jewels, the capital city is the host of many annual world-wide spectacles such as The Edinburgh Festival Fringe (the largest art festival in the world), the illusive Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo and the Edinburgh Hogmanay Street Party.

The Office

Providing immediate access to one of the best indoor climbing arenas in the world, olympic-standard gym facilities, indoor soft play complex, Judo Scotland National Training Centre and multiple cafes, there is plenty to do when you're not making use of our mindfulness pods or taking time away from our standing hot desks. Based less than 5-miles from Edinburgh Airport, no matter how far you want to travel, the first step of your journey is right on our doorstep.
