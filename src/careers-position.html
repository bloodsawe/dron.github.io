Geospatial 3D Processor

Apply

Location: Ratho, Edinburgh, EH28 8AA

Salary: Competitive, DOE

Contract: Full time, Permanent

Benefits: Subsidised Lunches, Free gym membership to Excite gym in Edinburgh Indoor Climbing Arena, Discounted Climbing Rates and a great Employee Benefits Scheme!

We are Cyberhawk and we are recruiting.

Cyberhawk is a fast-growing successful company with big ambitions. We already punch way above our weight and we are looking for someone to throw some punches with us!

We are industry leaders in providing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle inspection and survey solutions around the world but we are also becoming increasingly recognised as a world leader in visual asset management software solutions through our own iHawk cloud-based software.

In short - we do cool stuff and we are really good at it.

We recently moved into a stunning new office location on the upper floor of the EICA Climbing Centre at Ratho – Europe's largest indoor climbing arena. Currently employing around 60 people but with plans to grow aggressively in the coming years, it's an exciting place to be and we want you.

We are focused on building strong, ethical relationship with our clients, that highlight the values that underpin our business. You will need to take ownership of your tasks while playing your part of the team within the Company.

We are looking for a Geospatial 3D Processor who can multi-task as our business develops. You will be reporting to the Visualisation & Geospatial Manager; this role will primarily involve photogrammetric processing and analysis.

Our employees are our most valuable asset.

We want you to feel valued and you will be valued. You will receive a competitive basic salary commensurate with experience. You will be entitled to 30 days holiday per year (inclusive of Bank Holidays).

We are ideally looking for candidates to be situated within the central belt of Scotland but would consider remote working for the right candidate.

Geospatial 3D Processor - Responsibilities:

  • Using photogrammetry software to process imagery and LIDAR data to produce high quality 3d mesh models
  • Processing aerial/terrestrial imagery using photogrammetric processing software
  • Processing geospatial data
  • Image editing using Adobe Photoshop or similar
  • Creating virtual tours
  • Liaising with operational and technical managers

The Ideal Candidate:

Our employees are talented people, distinguished by technical excellence, with a willingness to embrace team working and a passion for client service. You will be able to demonstrate a similar supportive, flexible and driven ethos.

Essential

  • Previous experience with creating 3D mesh models
  • Previous experience with photogrammetry software
  • Previous experience handling geospatial data

Desired but not essential:

  • Previous experience with CAD or BIM software
  • Previous experience in image or video editing
  • An eye for detail and the ability to work to tight schedules
  • Excellent spelling and grammar and attention to detail
  • Strong time-management skills with the ability to prioritise tasks

If you feel you have the necessary skills and experience to be successful in this role click on “APPLY” today, forwarding an up to date copy of your CV for consideration in the first instance.

No agencies please.
