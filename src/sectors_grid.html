About Sector

Cyberhawk has been undertaking inspections, surveys and providing visual asset management solutions to the power grid industry since 2009.

Using a map-driven interface and traffic light color-coded reporting dashboard, iHawk allows easy access to inspection information and trends, with all inspection images and engineering commentary available. iHawk provides ‘one source of truth’, and the evidence required to make informed decisions.

Provided Features
World-Class Pilots

We employ some of the best drone pilots in the world, and the best pilots collect the highest quality data possible. Our exclusive in-house flight school extends to four levels of rigorous training which entails at least 18 months' experience and a minimum of 500 flights to develop our pilots into the world-class pilots who conduct Cyberhawk's inspection flights.

Quicker, Safer, Smarter

Eliminate the need to work at height or in other hazardous environments, maintaining stand-off distance from operating asset and eradicate the requirement of manned helicopters. Cyberhawk's drone-based data collection service is cheaper and faster than traditional methods whilst providing a superior level of detailed data and service.

End-to-End Solution

As well conducting complex drone inspections, we work with clients to digitize visual inspection results and maximize the benefit of drone-captured data through our data management software, iHawk. We provide visualization tools, such as visual asset integrity matrix, internal & external visual tours, accurate digital twins, network mapping, IoT data and much more, hosted from out cloud-based solution. This enables better decision-making, communication, budgeting and planning.

Overhead Lines & Networks
Our team of industry-leading pilots and engineers are trained to the highest standard to perform full network inspections. Using Drones, we can capture high-quality visual data for all your decision-making needs. Using your existing defect standard, or creating a new one, data can be processed and managed by iHawk's reporting matrix and iHawk viewer, allowing complete situational awareness throughout your organization
Substations
iHawk Substation is a cloud-based visual asset management system for full inspection, maintenance and repair of all assets, components and parts at a substation. iHawk Substation allows for creation of work orders to conduct inspection or maintenance on all assets at a substation.
