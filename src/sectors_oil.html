Provided Features
Inspection & Data Collection

Cyberhawk conducts close visual and thermal inspections and surveys of high, live and difficult to reach structures, assets and facilities using drones. We work on offshore assets, such as platforms, semi-submersibles and FPSOs and at onshore refineries, petrochemical plants and COMAH sites. As well conducting complex drone inspections, we work with clients to digitize inspection results and maximize the benefit of drone-captured data. We provide visualisation tools, such as visual asset integrity management solutions and external visual tours, creating accurate digital twins of your assets which are delivered via our cloud-based software, iHawk. This enables better decision-making, communication, budgeting and planning.

iHawk Data Management Suite

iHawk is transforming asset and project management by digitizing complex visual inspection, survey and site visualisation data and hosting it in the cloud for easy access, sharing and interrogation. Developed by Oil, Gas and Petrochemical inspection engineers and project managers with years of industry experience, we equip clients with the ability to integrate data from multiple sources. iHawk can be adapted to work in conjunction with external software such as ERP or data monitoring solutions. iHawk Oil, Gas & Petrochemical provides cloud-based access to visual asset data for your staff and contractors, ensuring everyone works from the same up-to-date point of reference. By hosting in the cloud, this also means that historic data can be stored and easily accessed to compare data points and track/monitor the condition of known defects over time.

In-House Inspection

From Cyberhawk’s inspection centres, our in-house teams of industry engineers analyse the data collected to categorise and report on all of the visual data collected, in accordance with the agreed defect standard. Our analysis and quality assurance process ensures inspection accuracy and efficiency, as inspectors only need to view images once to fully analyse.

Flare Stacks
Cyberhawk has completed more than 500 thermal flare inspections. We can inspect flares both onshore and offshore while they are live and online, avoiding the requirement to shutdown. This means savings can run into many millions of dollars per inspection.
Offshore Decks & Risers
Our expert pilots are able to fly, fully manually, under the deck of an offshore production platform or drilling rig. From there, they can zoom in on areas of interest and conduct close visual inspections (CVI) of areas such as the underdeck, risers or splashzone.
Tank, Tanker & PFSO
Cyberhawk can undertake Drone internal inspections of large tanks on operational vessels such as FPSOs, bulk carriers and tankers.
Onshore Storage Tanks
Using a proven solution for UT testing, Cyberhawk can quickly, easily and safely provide fully API-653 and EEMUA-159 compliant internally and externally inspections.
