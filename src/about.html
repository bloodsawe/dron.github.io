About Company

With a safety-and-service-first approach, our world-leading team are trusted partners of Energy & Construction organisations around the globe.

Founded in 2008, Cyberhawk is the world leader in visual asset management using drones and software.

As an industry pioneer, we have completed more than 35 world firsts since our inception operating worldwide in more than 37 countries (and counting!) Boasting a solid combination of industry veterans, world-class pilots and highly skilled team of in-house software developers, Cyberhawk offers a true end-to-end asset data manage solution for the energy and construction industry.
