About Company

With a safety-and-service-first approach, our world-leading team are trusted partners of Energy & Construction organisations around the globe.

Founded in 2008, Cyberhawk is the world leader in visual asset management using drones and software.

As an industry pioneer, we have completed more than 35 world firsts since our inception operating worldwide in more than 37 countries (and counting!) Boasting a solid combination of industry veterans, world-class pilots and highly skilled team of in-house software developers, Cyberhawk offers a true end-to-end asset data manage solution for the energy and construction industry.

Cyberhawk Innovations
Cyberhawk is the world’s leading engineering company using Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) for aerial inspection and surveying, and a pioneer in the development of innovative visual asset management and maintenance software.
Cyberhawk is much more than a drone company. We don’t leave you with a book of photographs; as well as conducting close visual and thermal inspections and surveys of industrial assets, both on and offshore, we also provide customers with a full, detailed inspection report or a digital, cloud-based visual asset management system.
We are committed to becoming a Co2 neutral business by 2021.
We estimate our business travel total is 184 tonnes of Co2 per year which can be offset by planting 8,500 trees and by actively making energy-conscious changes. By 2021 we plan to decrease our carbon emissions through the use of cleaner cars, car sharing, cycling scheme & energy saving, hosting multiple tree planting days throughout the year and by budgeting funds for projects that generate certified emission reductions.
