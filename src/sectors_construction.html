About Sector

As the world’s most experienced drone-based inspection and data management company, Cyberhawk has completed more than 35,000 flights for over 300 clients.

Providing solutions for an extensive variety of multi-billion-dollar construction sites, Cyberhawk's team of industry veterans and software engineers have joined forces to create the most powerful data management software solution available. Harnessing the power of visual data, third-party plugins and IoT, iHawk streamlines the way the construction industry manages critical data and provides complete situational awareness.

Provided Services
Inspection & Data Collection

We use fixed wing and multirotor drones to acquire high resolution aerial images. This imagery is processed using advanced photogrammetry software to generate orthophotos, digital elevation models and panoramic images of a site. This type of imagery is commonly used for site design, health and safety inductions, evacuation plans, tendering information and as-built photography. Additionally, we use IoT, sensors and third-party plugins to collect a complete stream of site data, all hosted on the cloud for complete single source of truth

iHawk Data Management Suite

iHawk Construction allows project teams to visualize the entire operation of their construction site from conception to completion. Enabling cross-organizational and cross-vendor workflows, planning of future worksite activities and tracking project milestones allows for big data to be managed in a slick, intuitive and digestible way. The software allows users to visualize all data sources for large construction projects, including:
• Drone imagery – orthophotos and aerial spherical images
• 3D digital twins – point clouds and mesh models
• 360-degree imagery – ground and aerial
• RFID asset tracking
• Equipment usage monitoring
• Time lapse imagery with BIM overlay
• Geographical document and tag registries
• Real-time GPS vehicle tracking
• Traffic management
